George Yu, PhD, LAT

Hey guys, welcome to my site. I am a sport physiotherapist who specialized in the musculoskeletal rehabilitation and sport injury prevention. I am a professional athletic trainer licensed by the Department of Health, State of Washington. I worked at several places including Bethany Lutheran College and Seattle Children's Hospital.

I obtained PhD in rehabilitation science from the Department of Rehabilitation Medicine, University of Washington. My research is primarily focusd on the clinical gait analysis, anthropometrics' influence on neuromuscular control during physical activity, and enegy expenditure of locomotion. My research heavily relies on the methods of biomechanics.

I was interested in engineering and mathematics while i was an undergraduate kinesiology student. I took many math and engineering courses, all of which lead to my path in rehabilitation medicine and biomechanics. I obtained MS in applied mathematics from the university washington with specialization in data science and dynamic system analysis.
